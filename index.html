2025 Santa Bob Appearance Dates
 

“JESUS IS THE REASON FOR THE SEASON”
Is the main thought in my mind in becoming Santa Bob!   

 

The 2024 season was marked with a lot of uncertainty.  I had a terrible accident in January of 2024 and was uncertain that I would even walk again.  But God was so good to me and through lots of prayer from family and friends and extensive therapy, I did recover in time enough to have another great year of being Santa.  I had the delightful opportunity to interact with numerous children, making it the most gratifying season yet. As in previous years, additional events were incorporated, such as the Christmas Festival at Hidden Haven in Milner, GA, photo shoots, schools, assisted living homes, Chik Fila Locations, Zaxby locations Those along with the best place ever to be Santa, my home during the Christmas Season, Rhodes Hall, The Castle on Peachtree.  Children have been coming to see me at Rhodes Hall for 8 years now and truly believe that is my home during Christmas.   

As always, the expressions of joy on the children's faces were profoundly touching and inspirational. It truly felt like a return to the essence of Christmas for children of all ages. Of special importance were the interaction heartwarming experience in itself.

In 2025 

I will be entering my 11th year as Santa;  and I am just as excited with anticipation of seeing all those children as the years before;the year promises to be the best every; vibrant and bustling, featuring fresh events accessible to everyone.

The calendar is also brimming with private parties and events.  

For me, Santa Claus epitomizes love, hope, and generosity for all individuals, especially the young ones. Witnessing the radiant smiles on children's faces is an unparalleled blessing. Throughout my life, I have engaged with children through sports, church activities like drama and music, Sunday School and Bible School – roles I've undertaken since I was old enough to contribute in those capacities. With 60 years of marriage, two children, and five grandchildren and now 1 great-grandson  I am a dedicated member of my church, deeply committed to addressing global needs. 

In the upcoming season, I will once again do my utmost to embody the Spirit of Christmas, nurturing imagination, and instilling hope in everyone I encounter, I am a dedicated member of my church, deeply committed to addressing global needs.
